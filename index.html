Good to know

Questions I get asked a lot

Is domain and hosting included?

No. Every website needs a domain name (for example, yourbusiness.com) and web hosting to keep it online. These are yearly services paid directly to the hosting provider. I'll help you choose the right option and guide you through the setup.

I'm not techy at all. Will I be able to keep up?

Completely. I explain everything in plain language, and I'll never assume you know technical terms. Most of my clients aren't tech people — that's exactly who I build for.

How long does a website take?

A simple page usually takes about a week. A full website is typically 2–4 weeks, depending on how quickly we can gather your content together. I'll give you a clear timeline before we start.

What do I need to prepare before we start?

Nothing fancy — just whatever you already have. A few photos, your business hours, prices if relevant, and a rough idea of what you'd like visitors to do (call you, visit, message you). I'll guide you through the rest.

What if I need changes after the website is done?

That's completely normal, and I expect it. Every project includes a few rounds of revisions, and afterwards you can ask about the Care & Updates plan or simply message me for a one-off fix.

Do you only build for businesses based in Malaysia?

Most of my clients are local, but I'm happy to work with small businesses anywhere, as long as we can communicate comfortably online.

What if I'm honestly on a tight budget?

Tell me anyway. I'll be upfront about what's realistic, and where possible, I'll suggest a smaller scope that still gets you something you're proud of — rather than turning you away.
